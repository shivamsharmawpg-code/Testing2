Beak-a-boo Park Trail

Interactive Map

Details

Follow the Seine River Greenway to spot woodpeckers, chickadees, and warblers along the riverbank. The trail is stroller-accessible, includes picnic benches, and has gentle elevation changes. Bring your Beak-a-boo kit to log sightings and compare notes with the catalogue.

  • Length: 2.8 km out-and-back
  • Surface: Packed gravel
  • Best season: Spring through early fall
  • Parking: 1015 Cottonwood Rd, Winnipeg
