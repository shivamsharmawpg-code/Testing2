Why birdwatch with Beak-a-boo in Winnipeg?

Beak-a-boo is a Winnipeg student-run brand focused on making Manitoba birdwatching approachable for families, classrooms, and beginners. We combine local trail recommendations, species learning tools, and practical quickstart resources so your first outing feels simple and fun.

Best places to go birdwatching in WPG

St. Vital Park Area

Great for beginners with walkable paths, mixed habitat, and regular activity from common songbirds.

Kildonan Park Area

River-edge habitat helps birders spot seasonal variation and practice identifying birds by sound.

Neighbourhood Green Spaces

Short local outings are perfect for families building a first list of Winnipeg species.

Birdwatching Winnipeg FAQs

Where should beginners birdwatch first in Winnipeg?

Start with shorter trails and parks where you can stop often, listen for calls, and practice using our catalogue as a field reference.

What time of day is best for birdwatching in WPG?

Early mornings are usually best for activity and bird calls, especially in spring and early summer.
